Enable or Disable Song Previews

Supports PSARCs, Sloppaks and loose folder songs.

Missing Song Previews

Some songs don't have a preview clip yet, so nothing plays when you hover over them. This tool creates the missing previews for you, picking the best method automatically:

  • Copy from PSARC — if the song's matching .psarc file is in your DLC folder and has a preview clip (most do), that clip is copied over. Best quality.
  • Generate — otherwise a 30-second clip is made from the song's own audio (starts near the first vocal or the catchiest part, with a short fade in and out).

Open Song Preview from the Plugins menu to see every song, what will happen to each, and fix them one at a time.
